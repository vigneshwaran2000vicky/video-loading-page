THALAPATHI VIJAY

Leo is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed
by Lokesh Kanagaraj and produced by Seven Screen Studio. The film
stars Vijay in the titular role, alongside Sanjay Dutt, Arjun, Trisha,
Gautham Vasudev Menon, Mysskin, Madonna Sebastian, George Maryan,
Mansoor Ali Khan, Priya Anand and Mathew Thomas.
